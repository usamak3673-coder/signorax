Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Are No U-Turn signs offensive?

No. No U-Turn signs are standard traffic regulatory signs intended for road safety. They are not offensive and are designed to control traffic flow and prevent accidents.

2. What is a No U-Turn sign?

A No U-Turn sign (R3-4) is a regulatory traffic sign that prohibits drivers from making a U-turn at a specific intersection or roadway. It helps improve traffic flow and safety.

3. Can you U-turn if there is no sign?

In the absence of a No U-Turn sign, U-turns are generally allowed unless restricted by local traffic laws or other regulations. Drivers must always ensure it is safe to perform a U-turn.
