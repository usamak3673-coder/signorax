Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What does a Wrong Way sign mean?

A Wrong Way sign warns drivers that they are traveling against the direction of traffic and must stop and turn around safely.

2. What is the MUTCD code for the Wrong Way sign?

The MUTCD code for the standard Wrong Way sign is R5-1a.

3. What color background does a Wrong Way sign have?

The Wrong Way sign has a red background with white lettering according to MUTCD standards.

4. Is the Wrong Way sign a regulatory sign?

Yes. The Wrong Way sign is a regulatory traffic sign and must be obeyed by law.

5. Where are Wrong Way signs usually placed?

Wrong Way signs are commonly installed at highway exit ramps, one-way streets, and divided roadways.

6. What should a driver do when they see a Wrong Way sign?

Drivers should stop immediately and turn around safely as soon as possible.

7. What is the difference between Wrong Way and Do Not Enter signs?

Wrong Way signs warn drivers they are moving against traffic, while Do Not Enter signs prohibit entry.

8. Are Wrong Way signs used only in the United States?

The R5-1a design is specific to the U.S. MUTCD, though similar signs exist worldwide.

9. Can I edit the Wrong Way sign vector files?

Yes. EPS and SVG files are fully editable and scalable without loss of quality.

10. Are these files suitable for large-format printing?

Yes. Vector files can be printed at any size while maintaining sharp quality.
