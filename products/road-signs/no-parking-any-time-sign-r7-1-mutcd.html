Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What does a double arrow No Parking Any Time sign mean?

A double arrow No Parking Any Time sign means that parking is prohibited at all times in both directions from the sign. The restriction applies to the area between the two arrow points or until another parking sign changes the rule.

Is stopping allowed at a No Parking Any Time sign?

No. A No Parking Any Time sign means vehicles may not park or stop at any time, except in emergencies or when required by traffic conditions.

Does a No Parking Any Time sign apply at night and on weekends?

Yes. The restriction is enforced 24/7, including nights, weekends, and public holidays, unless another sign clearly limits the hours.

How far does a No Parking Any Time sign apply?

The restriction usually applies from the sign’s location until the next parking sign, an intersection, or the end arrows shown on the sign.

What is the purpose of a No Parking Any Time sign?

The sign is used to keep roads clear for traffic flow, emergency access, and public safety, especially near intersections, fire lanes, and driveways.

Is the No Parking Any Time sign a regulatory sign?

Yes. It is a regulatory traffic sign that drivers must obey under traffic laws and MUTCD guidelines.
